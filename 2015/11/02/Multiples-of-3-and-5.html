Multiples of 3 and 5

02 November 2015 on project euler, python

Problem 1

If we list all the natural numbers below 10 that are multiples of 3 or 5, we get 3, 5, 6 and 9. The sum of these multiples is 23.

Find the sum of all the multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000.

El problema es bastante sencillo, basta con hacer una traducci√≥n casi literal del problema:

prje_1.py
#!/usr/bin/env python
N=1000
multiplo = lambda n: n if n%3==0 or n%5==0 else 0
print(sum(filter(multiplo,range(N))))
